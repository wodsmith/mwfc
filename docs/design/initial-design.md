# Mountain West Fitness Championship 2025
## Landing Page Design Brief

---

## Executive Summary

Transform the Mountain West Fitness Championship (MWFC) website into a premier digital experience for the **October 10-11, 2025** competition at the **Canyon County Event Center** in Caldwell, Idaho. Building on 2024's success of 360 athletes from 83 gyms across 18 states, the 2025 redesign must establish MWFC as the definitive fitness competition for the Mountain West region while targeting 400+ athletes.

**Key Partnership:** Presented by ProPath Financial | Programmed by Justin Medeiros

---

## Core Event Information

### 2025 Competition Details
- **Dates:** October 10-11, 2025 (Friday-Saturday)
- **Venue:** Canyon County Event Center - 42,000 square feet
- **Location:** Caldwell, Idaho
- **Expected Athletes:** 400+
- **2024 Performance:** 360 athletes | 83 gyms | 18 states
- **Instagram:** @mountainwestchampionship (5,290+ followers)

### Sister Event
- **Battle of the Ages:** January 17-18, 2025
- **Individual Masters Competition:** 200 athletes expected

## Visual Identity: Mountain Power Meets Athletic Excellence

### Typography Strategy
- **Primary Headlines:** Bold condensed fonts at 60-80px evoking mountain strength
- **Secondary Headers:** 32-48px for section titles
- **Body Text:** Clean, readable sans-serif at 16-18px
- **Font Personality:** Strong, angular typefaces suggesting elevation and peaks
- **Hierarchy:** Weight variations creating "elevation layers" in content

### Mountain-Inspired Color System

#### Primary Palette
- **Forest Green (#326549):** Deep mountain pine for headers and primary elements
- **Pitch Black (#000000):** Sharp contrast for text and bold statements
- **Summit White (#FFFFFF):** Clean backgrounds and breathing space

#### Accent Colors
- **Alpine Gray (#4A5568):** Secondary text and dividers
- **Sunrise Gold (#FFB700):** CTAs and urgency elements (Register Now buttons)
- **Ice Blue (#E0F2F1):** Success states and highlights
- **ProPath Red (#DC2626):** Sponsor integration accent

#### Color Application Rules
- Green dominates headers and key branded sections
- Black provides grounding and readability
- Gold creates urgency on CTAs
- White space represents "mountain air"

### Photography & Imagery Direction

#### Hero Imagery Requirements
- Athletes training with mountain/outdoor backdrops
- Wide shots of the 42,000sf Canyon County Event Center
- Dramatic low-angle shots suggesting upward climb
- Action shots from 2024 competition (360 athletes)
- Community moments showcasing 83 gym participation

#### Visual Metaphors
- **Mountain Peaks:** Achievement and personal goals
- **Elevation Lines:** Progress through divisions
- **Rocky Textures:** Strength and endurance
- **Trail Markers:** Navigation and journey
- **Summit Views:** Success and accomplishment

#### Image Treatment
- Dark gradient overlays (30-50% opacity) for text legibility
- Sharp, high-contrast editing emphasizing athletic intensity
- Green color grading to maintain brand consistency

### Layout Philosophy: "Alpine Architecture"

#### Design Principles
- **Vertical Emphasis:** Elements drawing eyes upward (mountain ascent)
- **Layered Content:** Information structured like elevation zones
- **Sharp Angles:** Diagonal section breaks at 5-10 degree angles
- **Geometric Patterns:** Mountain ridge inspired backgrounds
- **Strategic White Space:** 40-60px between major sections

#### Grid System
- 12-column grid for desktop (1440px container)
- 4-column grid for tablet
- Single column for mobile
- Consistent 20px gutters

## Mobile-First Requirements

### Critical Mobile Features

#### Above-Fold Priorities (iPhone 12 viewport)
1. MWFC Logo and date
2. "Register Now" CTA
3. Event countdown timer
4. Key trust signal (360 athletes)

#### Touch-Optimized Elements
- Minimum 44x44px touch targets
- 8px minimum spacing between interactive elements
- Swipeable division cards
- Thumb-reachable bottom navigation

#### Mobile-Specific Features
- One-tap venue directions (Google Maps integration)
- Add to calendar functionality
- Offline schedule download (PDF)
- Quick contact: Call/Text competition director
- Social sharing tools

### Performance Targets
- First Contentful Paint: <1.5s
- Largest Contentful Paint: <2.5s
- Time to Interactive: <3.5s
- Cumulative Layout Shift: <0.1

## Accessibility Standards

### Minimum Requirements
- **Color Contrast:** 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text

- High contrast mode toggle
- Text size adjustment controls
- Reduced motion option

## Final Design Principles

### "Summit Mindset"
Every design element should inspire athletes to push higher, reach further, and conquer their personal mountains.

### "Base Camp Community"
While celebrating individual achievement, emphasize the supportive community that makes the climb possible.

### "Mountain Time"
Respect the regional culture – straightforward, authentic, no-nonsense design that delivers information efficiently.

### "Elevation for Everyone"
From intermediate to elite, every athlete should feel this competition is within reach while still being aspirational.

### "ProPath to Success"
Integrate sponsor messaging naturally while maintaining focus on the athlete experience.
